Know Your Legal Rights

Learn about laws related to divorce, domestic violence, property rights and more.

Right Against Domestic Violence

Under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005, women have the right to protection from physical, emotional, economic and verbal abuse within the household.

Right to Divorce

Under the Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 a woman can file divorce on grounds such as cruelty, adultery, desertion, conversion, mental disorder or mutual consent.

Right to Maintenance

According to Section 125 of the Criminal Procedure Code, a woman can claim financial maintenance from her husband if he neglects or refuses to support her.

Right to Streedhan

Streedhan includes gifts, jewellery, money or property given to a woman before or after marriage. It legally belongs only to the woman.

Right to Child Custody

Under the Hindu Minority and Guardianship Act, 1956, courts prioritize the welfare of the child when deciding custody, often granting custody to the mother.

Right to Residence

Under the Domestic Violence Act, a woman has the right to reside in the shared household even if she does not own the property.
